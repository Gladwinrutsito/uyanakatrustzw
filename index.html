About Uyanaka Trust

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support to vulnerable children and orphans in Zimbabwe, ensuring they have access to quality education, proper nutrition, and the care they need to thrive and become productive members of society.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to receive an education, proper nutrition, and grow up in a caring environment that nurtures their potential.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Transparency, Community Partnership, and Sustainable Impact. We believe in working closely with communities to create lasting positive change.

Our Programs

Education Support

We cover school fees, provide uniforms, textbooks, and essential school supplies to ensure children can attend and succeed in school. Our education program has helped over 500 children stay in school.

Supplemental Feeding

Our nutrition program provides daily meals and nutritional supplements to malnourished children, ensuring they have the energy and nutrients needed for healthy growth and learning.

Healthcare Support

We provide basic healthcare services, medical checkups, and health education to ensure children maintain good health and can focus on their education and development.
